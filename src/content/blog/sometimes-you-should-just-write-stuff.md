---
title: Sometimes you should just write stuff
date_pub: 2026.04.28
---

I've locked my ass in a cage for about 2 years, while claiming that I'm actually chasing a "standard of quality".

I've been writing (almost) daily in [my digital journal](https://cocotier.ro/blog/you-should-really-start-a-journal/) for the past **1.476 days** (at the time of writing). Yet, if you look at my blog, my latest post is from almost a year ago. The post before that? Almost a year older than the last.

Across the 2 posts I've written in the past 2 years, there's around 1.000 words (according to a random word counter online). That's 1.3 words per day for 2 years. I CAN DO BETTER THAN THAT! I've been doing better than that. Over the same period, I've been writing hundreds of words in my journal. EVERY. DAY.

So why haven't I been doing the same for my blog? Amazing question, thanks for asking. Well, I could pretend that I just had a lot on my plate, and for certain periods of time that was true. In reality, I was just pretending that I have a "standard of quality" that I'm chasing, and every idea I had was just "not good enough" (or it required "too much effort").

I wouldn't consider myself a perfectionist, but I still strive for the stuff that I put into the world to be "good enough".

If you look over my [older posts](https://cocotier.ro/blog/), you'd be excused to think otherwise. Though, for that time, that's what I considered to be "quality". My standards did change since then, and now I kinda feel bad that I don't have more of these markers of how I evolved over the years.

Even if I don't really like those older posts now as much as I did when I wrote them, I want to keep them around as a reminder of how much I've grown.

So why am I writing now? After all this time, what changed? To put it plainly, I was a little inspired by reading some stuff on [BearBlog](https://bearblog.dev/discover/). I genuinely find it so nice when people share seemingly mundane stuff. I want to contribute to that. I want to write about **_my_** mundane stuff, and I hope other people will feel nice when they read it.

I'll try to write a bit more. I hope that I'll succeed.
