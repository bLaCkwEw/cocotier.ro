I actually finished the main story a couple of weeks ago. I played the game on and off for about a year or so. It is a great game, really well written, especially the character relationships. I got to say though, at some point it did feel a bit repetitive. But at the same time, even with the main story done, there's still a bunch of stuff that I could still continue to do. Lots of replayability packed into this one. I highly recommend it.
